How to Prepare Your Files for Analysis

Follow these simple steps to create and upload your CRM data for the sales pipeline analysis.

Pipeline File

Please download data with your current pipeline data from your CRM. Ensure the file contains the following fields:

Field NameDescriptionExample
Account NameName of the client or company.Company1
Created DateDate the opportunity was created.2024-05-01
Opportunity NameUnique name of the opportunity.Deal1
Opportunity OwnerName of the user managing it.User1
StageCurrent stage of the opportunity.Negotiation
TypeType of opportunity (e.g., New Customer, Renewal).New Customer
Net-New DollarsValue of the deal.$50,000
Close DateExpected or actual close date.2024-06-30
AgeDuration since creation.60 days
Discovery DateDate the opportunity was discovered.2024-04-01

File Format: Accepted File Types: .csv or .xlsx

Opportunity History File

Please download data with your opportunity history from your CRM. Ensure the file contains the following fields:

Field NameDescriptionExample
Opportunity NameUnique name of the opportunity.Deal1
To StageStage the opportunity transitioned to.Closed Won
From StageStage the opportunity transitioned from.Negotiation
AmountValue of the deal.$50,000
Probability (%)Probability of closing the deal.90%
Close DateExpected or actual close date.2024-06-30
Last ModifiedDate when the record was last updated.2024-06-01
Last Modified ByName of the user who last updated it.User1
OwnerName of the owner managing the opportunity.User2

File Format: Accepted File Types: .csv or .xlsx

Download Opportunity History Template

Upload Instructions

  1. Click on the Pipeline Upload button and select your file.
  2. Click on the Opportunity History Upload button and select your file.
  3. Use the date selector to define your analysis period.
  4. Click Generate Chart to view your sales pipeline waterfall chart.
